Ruby Belgium

The Association.

The Association aims at promoting the programming language Ruby and supporting the Belgian Ruby community. In that way, the Association organizes and supports events gathering Ruby developers and promoting Ruby. It works in Belgium for education, jobs, and spread of Ruby.

The association is more about the people than the technology.
